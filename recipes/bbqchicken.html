BBQ Chiken

Its BBQ chiken. Do you really need a description?

Ingredients

  • chiken thighs
  • Favorite BBQ suce
  • Additional seasonings if you want to be fancy

Instructions

  1. Put a little bit of the BBQ sauce in your instant pot so the chicken doesnt stick.
  2. Put your chicken into the pot
  3. Pour the rest of the BBQ sauce over the chiken.
  4. Sprinkle in additional seasonings if you want.
  5. put the lid on your instant pot and use the slow cook function.
