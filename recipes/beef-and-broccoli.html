Beef and Broccoli

You cant eat BBQ chicken every day. lets mix it up with some asian sturfry kinda thing

Ingredients

  • The cheapest cuts of meat you can find
  • Broccoli
  • Rice
  • soy sauce
  • teriyaki sauce
  • Whatever seasonings you want but absoulutly add ginger
  • I look to cook my rice in chicken broth so that optional

Instructions

  1. Put your rice in the instant pot with your chicken broth and cook that with the rice setting
  2. If your meat isnt already sliced go ahead and do that now
  3. Throw your meat into your wok with all the sauces and seasonings you want
  4. when your beef is almost completly cooked throw your broccoli in with it.
  5. When your broccoli is finished the rice should also be done
  6. Feast
